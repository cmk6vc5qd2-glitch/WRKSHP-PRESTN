Session Activities

Session
Activities

Four activities across the day. Each is designed to produce a concrete output. Use the dropdowns to see what each session involves and what you'll be working on.

01

Phase 03 · Pain Mapping

Where Does Your Workflow Break?

75 min · Groups of 3–4

Setup

  • Post-it notes + large paper per group
  • Groups: mix brands, not same-brand clusters
  • Timer visible to all
  • One wall per group for output

Steps

  • Individual: 8 mins silent writing — one pain per note
  • Group share-out: each person reads their notes aloud
  • Group clusters: group similar pains together, name the clusters
  • Group ranks top 3 by impact + frequency
  • Full room: each group presents, you cluster on master board

Discussion Prompts

  • "Where do you waste the most time in a typical design week?"
  • "What do you do manually that feels like it shouldn't be manual?"
  • "Where do decisions stall or get delayed?"
  • "What do you repeat every season that never changes?"
  • "Where does communication break between design and production?"

02

Phase 04 · Use Case Generation

Use Case Cards

75 min · Groups of 3–4

Card Format

  • Pain: What problem does this solve?
  • AI Function: What does the AI actually do?
  • Output: What does the designer receive?
  • Who Benefits: Which roles, which brands?
  • Current Workaround: How is this done today?

Steps

  • Each group takes 2 pain points from the master map
  • Generate as many use cases as possible — no filtering (15 min)
  • Select the 2 strongest to complete as full cards (20 min)
  • Gallery walk: all cards on wall, everyone reads (10 min)
  • Add reaction stickers: star = want this, question mark = unclear

Discussion Prompts

  • "If AI removed the most repetitive task in your design process — what would you use that time for instead?"
  • "What would a 10x faster version of this step look like?"
  • "What information do you always have to search for manually?"
  • "What do you explain to suppliers or stakeholders repeatedly?"

03

Phase 05 · Prioritization

Impact × Readiness Matrix

75 min · Full Room

Setup

  • Large 2×2 matrix on wall: X = Readiness, Y = Impact
  • All use case cards from Phase 04 ready to place
  • Dot stickers for voting (5 per person)
  • Designers and tech/IT must discuss together before placing

Steps

  • Dot vote first — 5 mins, no discussion
  • Top 7–8 use cases identified by vote count
  • Full group: place each on the matrix together (tech + design discuss)
  • Name the quadrants: Quick Wins / Strategic Bets / Explore Later / Drop
  • Agree on top 3–4 recommendations as a room

Expected Quadrant Results

  • Quick Wins: image generation for briefing, AI copy for product descriptions
  • Strategic Bets: sample-free digital approval, AI trend integration
  • Explore Later: full generative design, supplier AI integration
  • Drop: anything requiring data infrastructure not yet in place

04

Phase 06 · Community

Building the Network

45 min · Full Room

Purpose

  • Give the group an identity and a reason to continue
  • Establish lightweight governance (no bureaucracy)
  • Make the next meeting inevitable, not optional
  • Position you as the central node — not the gatekeeper

Questions to Facilitate

  • "What should this group be called?"
  • "How often should we meet? In what format?"
  • "What should we share between sessions?"
  • "Who wants to be the point of contact for their brand?"
  • "What's the one thing that would make this community worth showing up for?"

Close With

  • Name confirmed and written on the wall
  • Brand representatives noted (voluntary)
  • Format agreed: monthly / bi-monthly
  • Next session: date proposed before people leave
  • You: "I will send a summary + the tech recommendation brief within [X] days"
